Thanksgiving Prep Made Easy

Welcome! My name is Lauren and I am here to help you hose your next Thanksgiving dinner -- stress free!

Thanksgiving is a time to gather with family and friends and to share in food, drink, story, and tradition. Here is your start guide to planning a fantasic day.

To-Do List

Here are the main steps that you will need to take to host Thanksgiving:

  1. Make a guest list
  2. Prepare the Menu and Shop
  3. Prepare a Timeline
  4. Decorate and Clean/Declutter

Other things you can do to promote a stress-free holiday:

  • Make it a "pitch-in" thanksgiving -- you don't need to cook it all yourself!
  • Choose simpler recipes over difficult ones
  • Invite family the night before to assist with cooking, cleaning, etc.
